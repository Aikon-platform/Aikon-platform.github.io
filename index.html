About Aikon

Aikon is a modular platform that helps humanities scholars apply AI and computer vision to heritage collections: it offers a user-friendly interface for visualizing, extracting, and analyzing illustrations from documents. Built on proven technologies and interoperable formats, Aikon's adaptable architecture supports projects involving visual materials. It fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, resource sharing, and sustainability across digital humanities projects.

Overview

Key Features

  • Description and visualization of historical sources
  • Custom AI models trained for heritage documents
  • Automatic results exploration and edition
  • Modular architecture for different purposes
