About Aikon

Aikon is a modular platform designed to empower humanities scholars in leveraging artificial intelligence and computer vision methods for analyzing large-scale heritage collections. It offers a user-friendly interface for visualizing, extracting, and analyzing illustrations from historical documents, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and sustainability across digital humanities projects. Built on proven technologies and interoperable formats, Aikon's adaptable architecture supports projects involving visual materials. It fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, resource sharing, and sustainability across digital humanities projects.

Key Features

  • Description and visualization of historical sources
  • Custom AI models trained for heritage documents
  • Automatic results exploration and edition
  • Modular architecture for diverse research purposes
